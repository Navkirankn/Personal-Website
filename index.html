About Me

Culture

Both of my Parents are Punjabi. I love being Indian because we have the best food (shahi paneer and garlic naan = the BEST combo), best parties(entire week long weddings) and best outfits. I love Indian attire and getting dressed up in lenghas and Punjabi suits makes me feel so royal and elegant. I have taken classes to play the harmonium one of the religious instruments in our culture.

School life

While in School I always had an interest towards math. It was my favorite subject since I scored a perfect score on the math portion of the state test back in 3rd grade (we had a whole award ceremony and everything!). My math teachers have always been my favorite and I would race with my friends to solve assigments the quickest. Since I had this interest I chose to go into engineering in the hopes that I would be able to expand my knowledge.
